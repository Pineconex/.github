## PineconeX

**Backtest and live-trade Pine Script v6 strategies.** PineconeX is an
independent interpreter and hosting platform for systematic traders: write a
strategy, backtest it against historical data, optimise it, then run it live
against your own broker account.

🌲 **[pineconex.com](https://pineconex.com)**

### What's here

This organisation hosts our public repositories — documentation, the wiki, and
other open resources. The platform itself is a hosted service; sign up at
[pineconex.com](https://pineconex.com).

### What PineconeX is — and isn't

PineconeX is a **technology and hosting provider**, not an investment firm. We
hold no client funds, give no advice, and place no trades on your behalf — you
trade your own broker account with strategies you author and control. Nothing
here is financial advice.
