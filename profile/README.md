## PineconeX

**Backtest and live-trade Pine Script v6 strategies.** PineconeX developed it's own proprietary interpreter and provides hosting platform for systematic traders: write a
strategy, backtest it against historical data, optimise it, then run it live
against your own broker account.

PineconeX maintains the Pine Script v6 interpreter, written in Rust 🦀.

🌲 **[pineconex.com](https://pineconex.com)**

### What's here

This organisation hosts our public repositories — documentation, the wiki, and
other open resources. The platform itself is a hosted service; sign up at
[pineconex.com](https://pineconex.com).

### What PineconeX is — and isn't

PineconeX has its own independent interpreter and provides hosting — it is a
**technology and hosting provider**, not an investment firm. We hold no client
funds, give no advice, and exercise no discretion — every trade
is generated by a strategy you author, enable, and control, and is executed
against your own broker account. Nothing here is financial advice.
